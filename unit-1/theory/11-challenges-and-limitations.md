# 11. Challenges and Limitations

Despite their capabilities, LLMs have limitations.

## Hallucinations

Generating incorrect information confidently.

## Bias

Reflecting biases present in training data.

## Privacy Concerns

Sensitive information leakage risks.

## Computational Cost

Large models require significant hardware resources.

## Explainability

Difficult to understand internal decision-making.
